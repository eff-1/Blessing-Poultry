# GALLERY IMAGES

Put your gallery images here. Mix of farm life, products, facilities, and activities.

## Required Images (20-40 images):

Name them: **gallery-1.jpg** through **gallery-40.jpg**

## What to Include:
- Farm life photos
- Different bird breeds
- Facilities and coops
- Products (eggs, chickens, etc.)
- Happy customers (if they agree)
- Farm activities
- Behind-the-scenes
- Clean environment shots
- Team at work
- Equipment and technology
- Day-to-day operations
- Special events

## Image Specs:
- Size: 1200x900px (landscape or square)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Quality: Good resolution
- File size: Under 400KB each

## Tips:
- Variety is key!
- Mix of close-ups and wide shots
- Show different aspects of your farm
- Professional but authentic
- Good lighting
- Tell your farm's story through images
